Justin Lidard

Research Scientist at Google DeepMind. I build robots that improve beyond their training data — learning from their own experience to reason, plan, and act safely in the real world. Recently, I've been thinking about how to train robot policies using large amounts of video data.

Previously, I completed my PhD at Princeton, where I worked with Anirudha Majumdar and Naomi Leonard on robotics, and spent time at the Toyota Research Institute working on human-interactive driving.
