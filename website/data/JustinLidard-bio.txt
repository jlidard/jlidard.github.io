Justin Lidard is a Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering PhD student at Princeton
University, where he works in the intersection of dynamical systems and machine
learning. He recieved his B.S. in aerospace engineering with a minor in computer 
science at the University of Maryland, where he completed honors thesis research 
on maximizing airfoil lift with nonlinear control of an aerodynamic regression 
model with Professor Derek Paley. He recieved the UMD Rivello Award for Academic 
Excellence in 2019, the NASA Space Technology Research Fellowship in 2020, and 
the United States Department of Defense National  Defense Science and Engineering 
Graduate (NDSEG) Fellowship, also in 2020.
